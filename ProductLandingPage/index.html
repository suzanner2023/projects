Making Soy Candles With Natural Oils

Did you know that most decorative candles are made with petroleum-based synthetic fragrances that can include phthalates and cancer-causing chemicals? In this class you will blend natural essential oils to create your own poured soy candle. All materials will be provided.

Due to equipment limitations, class will be divided into two sessions, please select your preferred time when registering for the course. No walk-in registrations allowed.
